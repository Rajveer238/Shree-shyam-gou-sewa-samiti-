About Us

We are a compassionate Gou Shala operating as a hospital for injured, sick, and abandoned cows. Our mission is to provide medical care, shelter, and love to every cow that comes through our gates.

Our Services

Veterinary Care

Round-the-clock care including surgeries, medicines, and rehabilitation for injured and sick cows.

Emergency Response

Our team responds to calls about abandoned or injured cows within nearby villages and towns.

Sheltering

Clean, safe, and peaceful shelter for over 200 cows with proper feeding and hygiene.

Adopt or Sponsor a Cow

You can adopt or sponsor a cow to help cover its medical and food expenses. Receive updates, photos, and blessings!
